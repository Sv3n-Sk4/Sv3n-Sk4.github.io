REFRAMD — Privacy Policy

Last updated : 29 April 2026

REFRAMD is a mobile application that lets you discover, save and share Rive animations from the Rive Community. This privacy policy describes what (very little) data the app handles.

1. Data we do NOT collect

REFRAMD does not collect, store, transmit or share any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, address)
  • Account credentials (no signup, no login)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, advertising ID)
  • Health, financial, or biometric data
  • Contacts, calendar, photos, or any other on-device content
  • Behavioral analytics or usage tracking
  • Crash reports with personally identifiable information

2. Data stored locally on your device

The following information is stored only on your device, in the application's private sandbox, and is never transmitted to any server :

  • Your saved animations (favorites)
  • The list of animations you have already viewed (to avoid showing them again in Discover)
  • The list of animations you have hidden in Browse
  • Whether you have completed the onboarding
  • The last tag filter you selected in Browse

This data may be included in your device's iCloud or Google Drive backup if you have backups enabled — that mechanism is provided by Apple/Google and is outside our control.

3. Network requests

The app makes HTTP requests to the Rive Community API to fetch animation metadata and animation files (.riv). These requests do not include any user identifier — they are anonymous. The Rive Community service has its own privacy policy.

The app also loads the Rive runtime JavaScript file from unpkg.com CDN at first launch. unpkg.com may log the IP address making the request, per their standard CDN behavior.

4. Advertising

REFRAMD v1 contains no advertising. A future version may introduce non-personalized ads through Google AdMob, in which case this policy will be updated and consent will be requested where required by law (GDPR, CCPA).

5. Children's privacy

REFRAMD is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children, since we do not collect data from anyone.

6. Your rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing for us to provide, correct or delete on your behalf. To clear local data on your device, use Settings → "Reset complet" inside the app, or delete and reinstall the app.

7. Content licence

Animations displayed in REFRAMD come from the Rive Community and are licensed under CC BY 4.0. The original creator is credited inside the app. REFRAMD does not host or republish these animations beyond the in-app discovery and sharing flow you control.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the new version will be published at this URL and the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
