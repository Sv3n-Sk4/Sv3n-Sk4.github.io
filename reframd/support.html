Reporting an animation

Every animation screen in REFRAMD includes a "Report" action (flag icon). Reporting an animation immediately hides it from your feed and sends us an email report for review. You can also report content directly by emailing us the animation's title or a link to it.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account?
No. REFRAMD works fully without sign-up. Your favorites and albums are stored locally on your device.

Is REFRAMD affiliated with Rive?
No. REFRAMD is an independent, unofficial app that lets you browse the public Rive Community catalog. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Rive.

Why do I see ads?
REFRAMD is free. Non-personalized ads (Google AdMob) keep it that way — no subscription, no in-app purchases, no paywall.

How do I reset my data?
Open Settings inside the app for granular resets (Discover history, favorites) or a full factory reset that erases all local data at once.

Can I use these animations in my own project?
Yes — all content is licensed under CC BY 4.0. Every export and code snippet in the app automatically includes the required creator attribution.
